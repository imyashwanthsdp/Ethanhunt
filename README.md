# ♾️ EthanHunt

### Advanced AI-Powered Research & Intelligence Platform

EthanHunt is an AI-driven research platform engineered to transform complex queries into structured, context-aware insights through automated web intelligence gathering, content extraction, and large language model reasoning.

Designed as a next-generation research assistant, EthanHunt combines intelligent information retrieval, contextual memory, and AI-powered synthesis to deliver comprehensive responses in real time through an intuitive conversational interface.

---

## 🚀 Key Capabilities

* **AI-Powered Research Engine** — Generates detailed, context-aware insights from user queries.
* **Automated Web Intelligence Gathering** — Discovers and extracts relevant information from multiple online sources.
* **Contextual Session Memory** — Maintains conversation context for improved continuity and research depth.
* **Real-Time Knowledge Synthesis** — Converts scattered information into structured, actionable outputs.
* **Interactive Conversational Interface** — Modern chat-based workspace designed for research workflows.
* **Markdown & Mathematical Rendering** — Supports rich text formatting and mathematical expressions.
* **Research History Management** — Organizes and tracks previous explorations.
* **Scalable API Architecture** — Built for future expansion into advanced autonomous research workflows.
